## Why 77 Security?

As AI becomes integrated into every layer of software, the attack surface grows. Traditional firewalls don't stop **Prompt Injection**.

### Current Research Areas
1. **Adversarial Attacks:** How small changes to input can trick a model.
2. **Data Privacy:** Preventing models from "remembering" sensitive user data.

> "Security is not a product, but a process." — Bruce Schneier
