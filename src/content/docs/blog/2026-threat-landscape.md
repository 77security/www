The AI security landscape is shifting rapidly. This week, we saw the full impact of the **EchoLeak** vulnerability.

### What is EchoLeak?
EchoLeak is a zero-click vulnerability that exploits how AI assistants read emails. By hiding instructions in the "invisible" layers of an HTML email, attackers can steal data without the user ever knowing.

### How to Protect Your Team
1. **Sanitize AI Inputs:** Ensure your AI is not reading raw, untrusted data.
2. **Monitor API Activity:** Look for spikes in outbound traffic from your AI agents.
