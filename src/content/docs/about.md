## Our Mission
**77 Security** is an independent security research collective headquartered in **Europe**. Our mission is to bridge the gap between rapid AI innovation and the fundamental principles of cybersecurity. 

As AI Agents begin to handle sensitive data and execute autonomous tasks, the need for a "Security-First" approach has never been more critical. We provide deep-dive technical analysis, threat modeling, and defensive strategies for organizations navigating the 2026 AI threat landscape.

## Why a European Perspective?
Being based in the European Union, we are at the heart of the world's most robust AI regulatory environment. Our research is uniquely positioned to address:

* **Compliance & The EU AI Act:** We help organizations align their technical security with European legal frameworks.
* **Privacy by Design:** Rooted in the spirit of GDPR, we advocate for AI systems that respect user data sovereignty from the first line of code.
* **Digital Sovereignty:** We support the development of secure, transparent AI infrastructure that reduces dependence on non-transparent, black-box systems.

## What We Do
* **Adversarial Research:** Red-teaming LLMs and autonomous agents to find edge-case vulnerabilities.
* **Threat Intelligence:** Tracking emerging malware families like *Slopoly* that utilize generative AI.
* **Best Practices:** Developing open-source security standards for "Security for AI" (Protecting the models) and "AI for Security" (Using models to defend).
